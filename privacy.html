3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data under the following legal bases set out in GDPR Article 6:

  • E-book orders (name + email): Article 6(1)(b) — processing is necessary to fulfill the contract of delivering your purchased e-book.
  • Newsletter subscriptions (email): Article 6(1)(a) — explicit consent given when you submit your email address to subscribe. You may withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing.
  • Google Analytics: Article 6(1)(a) — consent given via the cookie banner. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or clicking "Decline" if you revisit the banner.
  • Google Fonts: Article 6(1)(f) — legitimate interest in providing a correctly rendered and accessible website.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Article 15): You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification (Article 16): You can request that we correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Article 17): You can request deletion of your data where no overriding legal obligation requires us to retain it.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Article 18): You can request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability (Article 20): You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Article 21): You can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent (newsletter, analytics), you may withdraw at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kubilaymt25@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
